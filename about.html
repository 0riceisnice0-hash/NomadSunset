About the Practitioner

My work is built upon a foundation of thirty-four years within Health and Social Care alongside my dedicated practise as a Holistic Practitioner. Spiritually aware since childhood I have spent my life bridging the practical needs of humanity with the subtle realms of spirit.

I am an individual who thrives in nature and views life as a sacred opportunity to create lovingly. My philosophy is to embrace our Flawesome human self. This means accepting our imperfections whilst consciously aligning to our highest qualities.

I hold mastery as a Reiki Practitioner since 2005 and operate under the professional standards of the IPHM.

Core Principles of the Work

To provide clarity on my methodology I work through three primary stages:

Blueprint

Recognising your unique spiritual signature and divine potential.

Structure

Refining the energetic and mental architecture that governs your daily experience.

Evolution

Supporting the continuous expansion of your consciousness as you move toward self realisation.
