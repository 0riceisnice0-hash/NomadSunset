Privacy and Policy

Booking and Payment Policy

I honour the IPHM professional guidelines and Code of Conduct. To ensure a committed and professional relationship please note the following:

Payment

All payments are required in advance or on the day of the session.

Refunds

I do not offer refunds for any services or digital products.

Cancellations

If you are unable to attend a scheduled session then your payment will be deferred to a future date.

Privacy Statement

As a wellbeing practitioner I treat all communications with the strictest confidence. Your personal details will only be used to manage your bookings and will never be shared with third parties. I aim to respond to all email enquiries within forty eight hours.

Data Collection

This is a static website and does not collect any personal data automatically. Any information you provide via email is used solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry and managing your bookings.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies. If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Third Party Services

Payment processing is handled through PayPal. Please refer to PayPal's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. Please contact us via email to exercise these rights.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please email nomadssunset888@gmail.com
